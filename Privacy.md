# Early Math Quest - Privacy Policy

**Last updated: May 2026**

---

## Information We Collect

Early Math Quest does not directly collect, store, or transmit any personal information. The app does not require account creation, login, or any personal data input.

---

## Third-Party Advertising (Google AdMob)

Early Math Quest displays banner advertisements and optional rewarded video advertisements provided by Google AdMob. Google AdMob may automatically collect:

- Device identifiers (IDFA, when permitted)
- IP address
- General location data (country/region level)
- App usage data and interaction with ads
- Device information (model, OS version, screen size)

Google uses this information to serve relevant advertisements. For more information on how Google handles data, please review:

- [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
- [How Google uses information from apps](https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites)

---

## App Tracking Transparency

On iOS 14.5 and later, Early Math Quest respects the App Tracking Transparency (ATT) framework. If you decline tracking permission, Google AdMob will serve non-personalized advertisements only.

---

## Children's Privacy

Early Math Quest is designed for users of all ages, including children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Advertisements shown to users who select the younger age groups are served using Google's child-directed settings when applicable.

---

## Data Retention

Early Math Quest does not store any user data on external servers. All quiz progress and preferences are stored locally on your device and are not transmitted to us.

---

## Your Rights

You have the right to:

- **Opt out of ad tracking** by declining the App Tracking Transparency prompt or by going to Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking on your iOS device
- **Reset your advertising identifier** by going to Settings > Privacy & Security > Apple Advertising
- **Delete all local data** by uninstalling the app from your device

---

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
